parasyte storyline

Parasyte centers on a male 17-year-old high school student named Shinichi Izumi,

who lives with his mother and father in a quiet neighborhood in Fukuyama, Hiroshima, Japan.

One night, tiny worm-like aliens with drill-like heads called Parasites arrive on Earth,

taking over the brains of their hosts by entering through their ears or noses.

One Parasite attempts to crawl into Shinichi's nose while he sleeps, but fails as Shinichi wakes

up,and enters his body by burrowing into his arm instead. In the Japanese version,

it takes over his right hand and is named Migi (ミギー), after the Japanese word for 'right'.Shinichi was able to prevent

Migi from traveling further up into his brain, both beings retain their separate intellect and personality.
